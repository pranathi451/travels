Forests

Forests in North India

Jim Corbett National Park

Location: Uttarakhand
Specialty: India's oldest national park, known for its Royal Bengal Tigers. Home to elephants, leopards, and rich flora. Offers jeep and elephant safaris.

Kanha National Park

Location: Madhya Pradesh
Specialty: Dense forest with diverse wildlife. Known for Bengal tigers, leopards, and barasingha. Inspiration for Rudyard Kipling's The Jungle Book.

Forests in South India

Periyar Wildlife Sanctuary

Location: Kerala
Specialty: Famous for its elephant and tiger reserve. Boating on Periyar Lake offers stunning forest views. Home to gaurs, leopards, and many bird species.

Bandipur National Park

Location: Karnataka
Specialty: Dense forest with diverse flora and fauna. Known for elephants, tigers, and sandalwood trees. Offers jeep safaris and nature walks.

Forests in West India

Gir Forest National Park

Location: Gujarat
Specialty: Famous for the Asiatic lions. Home to leopards, antelopes, and crocodiles. Jeep safaris offer wildlife sightings.

Tadoba Andhari Tiger Reserve

Location: Maharashtra
Specialty: Known for tigers, sloth bears, and wild dogs. Dense forest with bamboo and teak trees. Jeep safaris provide wildlife sightings.

Forests in East India

Sundarbans Mangrove Forest

Location: West Bengal
Specialty: Largest mangrove forest in the world. Home to the Royal Bengal Tigers and rich in biodiversity including saltwater crocodiles.

Kaziranga National Park

Location: Assam
Specialty: Famous for the one-horned rhinoceros. Home to elephants, wild buffalo, and swamp deer. Offers jeep and elephant safaris.

Temples

Temples in North India

Golden Temple (Harmandir Sahib)

Location: Amritsar, Punjab
Specialty: A Sikh pilgrimage site known for its gold-plated architecture and serene Amrit Sarovar.

Kedarnath Temple

Location: Uttarakhand
Specialty: Dedicated to Lord Shiva, part of the Chota Char Dham pilgrimage, nestled in the Himalayas.

Location: Uttarakhand
Specialty: Dedicated to Lord Vishnu and an integral part of the Char Dham pilgrimage, surrounded by majestic Himalayan peaks.
